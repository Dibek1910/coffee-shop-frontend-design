Cappuccino

Indulge in the creamy and frothy delight of our Cappuccino. This perfectly balanced coffee beverage combines shots of rich espresso with steamed milk and a layer of velvety milk foam, creating a luxurious and satisfying coffee experience.

Mocha

Treat yourself to the irresistible combination of chocolate and coffee with our Mocha. This indulgent beverage combines shots of espresso with rich chocolate syrup and steamed milk, topped with a dollop of whipped cream, delivering a heavenly taste experience.

Affogato

Discover the perfect harmony of hot espresso and cold ice cream in our Affogato. A scoop of premium vanilla ice cream is drowned in a shot of espresso, creating a delightful contrast of temperatures and flavors that will satisfy your cravings for both coffee and dessert.
